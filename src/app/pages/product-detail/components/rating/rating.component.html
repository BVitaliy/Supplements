Rating system

Our ratings are formed exclusively on such data as

Ingredient Impact: We evaluate each individual ingredient on factors such as benefit, weakness, contaminant, and allergen, combining all these ingredients and their effects we get an overall score

Manufacturer Transparency: The clarity and completeness of the information provided by the manufacturer about the product also play a crucial role in the rating.

Raitings levels

God: 8-10
Not bad: 4-7
Bad: 0-3

These factors combine to provide a reliable and informative rating to help users make informed decisions about their supplements.
